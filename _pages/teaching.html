Outreach

LiLu2 Tech Day: Data Science Workshops for High School Students (2025)

In 2025, I participated in the LiLu2 Tech Day at Liceo cantonale di Lugano 2, organizing and delivering hands-on workshops on data science for high school students. This activity is part of the Technology and IT Weeks program.

LaM: Support for High School Thesis Projects (Lavori di Maturità) (2025)

In 2025, I supported high school students with their Lavori di Maturità (LaM) one-year thesis projects in collaboration with the Faculty of Informatics at USI and the Liceo cantonale di Lugano 2 (LiLu2). The project currently under development can be viewed here. Read more.
