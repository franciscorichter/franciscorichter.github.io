\cvsubsection{LiLu2 Tech Day: Data Science Workshops for High School Students}{Lugano, Switzerland}{2025}
Organized and delivered hands-on data science workshops for high school students as part of the Technology and IT Weeks program at Liceo cantonale di Lugano 2.

\cvsubsection{LaM: Support for High School Thesis Projects (Lavori di Maturità)}{Lugano, Switzerland}{2025}
Supported high school students in their one-year thesis projects in collaboration with the Faculty of Informatics at USI and LiLu2, focusing on mentorship and project guidance.

\cvsubsection{Giornate Autogestite}{Ticino, Switzerland}{2025}
Participated in self-managed days at several high schools, offering workshops and activities related to data science, technology, and academic life, fostering dialogue between students and academia.
