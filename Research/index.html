I am currently developing my PhD project at the Johann Bernoulli Institute for Mathematics and Computer Science and the Groningen Institute for Evolutionary Life Sciences under the supervision of Rampal Etienne and Erns Wit

I am interested on study complex systems of ecological interactions in relationship with macro-evolutionary processes.

In particular I am focused on studying the following questions

  • How diversity influences diversification
  • At what extent we can infer evolutionary processes with the available information we get from nature?, how fast is that information growing?
  • How are asambles of species configured
