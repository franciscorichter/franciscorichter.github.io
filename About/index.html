Who Am I?

Hello! I'm Francisco, an applied mathematician with a passion for unravelling the mysteries of the universe, whether they're hidden within astronomical images or embedded in genetic data. Nestled in the beautiful city of Lugano, I've dedicated my life to using mathematics as a tool to solve intricate problems, spanning diverse fields from finance to ecology.
