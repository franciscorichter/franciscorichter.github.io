About

Francisco is a doctoral student in Mathematical Evolutionary Biology at Bernoulli Institute for Mathematics, Computer Science and Artificial Intelligence and the Groningen Institute for Evolutionary Life Sciences under the supervision of Rampal Etienne and Erns Wit . He received his diploma in Mathematical Engineering from the UTFSM, where he developed the thesis Mathematical modeling applied to photometric correction of astronomical observations under the supervision of Dr. Fernando Selman and Dr. Pedro Gajardo. Before his current position he worked as a data scientist for an international firm on the area of machine learning and stochastic modeling.
