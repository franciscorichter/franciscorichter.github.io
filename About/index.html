About

As an applied mathematician, Francisco thrives in solving complex problems across diverse fields, such as astronomical images, financial, ecological, and genetic data. He is passionate about developing AI methodologies and machine learning techniques.

Experience

Francisco has extensive experience, including roles as a Postdoctoral Researcher at USI Università della Svizzera italiana, Senior Research Analyst at Evalueserve, Research Associate at ESO European Southern Observatory, and Research Assistant at USM Universidad Tecnica Federico Santa Maria.

Skills

Algorithm design, complex systems modeling, statistical learning, AI methodologies, machine learning techniques, data visualization, and analysis.

Education

PhD in Biostatistics from the University of Groningen and a degree as Mathematical Engineer from Universidad Técnica Federico Santa María. Also attended KTH, Royal Technical University of Stockholm as an International Student.
