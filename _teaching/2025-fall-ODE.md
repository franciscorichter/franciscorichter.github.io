This course introduces the fundamental concepts and techniques of ordinary differential equations (ODEs), with a focus on applications in artificial intelligence and computational science. Topics typically include:

- First-order and higher-order ODEs
- Analytical and numerical solution methods
- Stability and qualitative behavior of solutions
- Applications to modeling in science and engineering

For more information, visit the [official USI course page](https://search.usi.ch/it/corsi/35275460/introduction-to-ordinary-differential-equations).
